BBQ Pizza Recipe

Description

How could one not like a sweet and savory BBQ Chicken Pizza? The combination of the cheese and sweet BBQ sauce with a dash of red onion is making me crave it by just typing this out right now. If you're interested in learning about how to make it, check out the recipe below!

Ingredients:

  • You're choice of Pizza Dough
  • Sweet Baby Rays BBQ Sauce (Or your favorite brand)
  • Grilled Chicken (Fried Chicken also works)
  • Shredded Mozzarella Cheese
  • Chopped Red Onion
  • Sausage
  • Bacon Bits
  • Oregano
  • Red Pepper Flakes

Steps:

  1. Expand the pizza dough into a circle with the size of your choice.
  2. Lightly spread your BBQ sauce over the the pizza dough with a spoon.
  3. Sprinkle a generous amount of shredded Mozzarella
  4. Spread your chicken evenly around the pizza
  5. Drizzle more bbq sauce around the pizza
  6. Chop your red onions and spread it around the pizza.
  7. Add your bacon bits and sausage
  8. Lastly, sprinkle some oregano
  9. Once you're pizza is ready to get placed in the oven, preheat it for 5 - 10 minutes
  10. Place your pizza into the oven for 400 degrees
